Apple Pie

How To Bake The Perfect Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • Pie crust
  • 6 cups peeled apples
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 2 table spoons flour
  • 3/4 table spoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tea spoon salt
  • 1/8 tea spoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 table spoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Pre heat the oven to 425 degrees, place the pie crust into a pan.
  2. Mix the ingredients in a bowl, and pour the mixture into the bowl.
  3. Put the top crust over the crust and the mixture.
  4. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes.
