Burger Recipe

How To Make The Best Burger

Ingredients

  • 80/20 Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Prep the ground beef into multiple burger patties
  2. Heat the cast iron skillet and melt the butter inside the skillet
  3. Cook the burgers for around 6 minutes and flip
  4. Cook both sides until the meat is cooked
