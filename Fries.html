How To Make Fries

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Vegtable oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes into frie shapes(a frie cutter makes it easy)
  2. Heat the oil to 375 degrees
  3. Put the fries in the oil and let it fry for about 6 minutes
  4. Take the fries out once golden and let them sit for a couple minutes
